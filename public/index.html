Growing More, Wasting Less

SproutEd is a hands-on plant kit and STEM challenge that helps students build better habits around food through gardening and play.

Our Mission

SproutEd started as a way to rethink food education and combat food waste by reconnecting students with the source of their food. It’s a tool to make healthy eating habits more approachable, gardening more rewarding, and sustainable practices easier.

How It Works

Each SproutEd kit includes everything you need to start growing your own plant and to compete on a live leaderboard. Sensor data from the plant updates in real-time, helping students stay engaged and motivated to care for their plant.
